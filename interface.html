App privacy questions requirement

Starting December 8, 2020, all new apps and app updates submitted to Apple will require information about your app’s privacy practices, including the practices of third-party partners whose code you integrate into your app.

After your app is submitted to the app store, an Apple user with Account Holder or Admin role needs to log in to App Store Connect and complete the privacy questions.

This only needs to be completed once each app and must be kept up to date.
